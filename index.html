Wanderer is a new app to find interesting places

Wanderer collects user-provided content to catalogue the most popular and intriguing sights near you. With an elegant user interface and streamlined Google Maps integration, Wanderer makes it straightforward to find the places people enjoy the most.

Features

Find Locations

With Wanderer, finding well-travelled and incredible points of interest takes seconds. Find locations in your area and discover a whole new world at your fingertips with your smartphone.

Add Sights

Know someplace amazing that deserves to be recognized? Add it to the Wanderer database and let the world know what awaits them.

Rate

When you visit points of interest, you can rate them to let others know if they are worth visiting or not. If a location receives a low enough rating, it will be removed.

Discover

Constantly changing and always growing, Wanderer gives you a new way to explore your surroundings, no matter where you are in the world.
